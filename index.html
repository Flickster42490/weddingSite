Venues

Our Wedding will be held at the South Seas Island Resort on beautiful Captiva Island, FL!

Ceremony - SUNSET COVE

Few beachfront wedding locations remain where jaw-dropping views exist at every turn. Sunset Cove is a secluded ceremony site where white sand meets the tranquil waters of the Gulf of Mexico. The sun sets on the horizon. Dolphins jump playfully within sight. Blue Heron soar above. Palm trees sway in the gentle breeze. You are surrounded by 360 degrees of stunning natural beauty.

Reception - KING'S CROWN LAWN

Enjoy a landmark experience. Kings Crown is the only free-standing building from the time when this iconic resort was first built in the 1880 s. It is truly the heart of South Seas. Restored but maintaining the charm of old Florida, King s Crown lawn overlooks Pine Island Sound and the T-Dock. Brides will marvel at the exquisite water view without the sand between her toes. The lawn is marked by towering Royal Palm trees and tropical foliage.
